**Date:** September 23, 2025

**Project:** SKIN-TWIN Hypergraph Network Analysis

## Executive Summary

This comprehensive research project successfully analyzed and updated the SKIN-TWIN supplier hypergraph network, covering **91 ingredients** across **23 South African suppliers**. The research identified over **2,400 skincare ingredients** available through the network, with **20 suppliers confirmed as operational** and **3 requiring direct contact verification**.

## Key Achievements

### 1. Comprehensive Supplier Research
- **100% coverage** of all 23 suppliers in the SKIN-TWIN network
- **Parallel processing** enabled efficient research across multiple suppliers simultaneously
- **Real-time data collection** from official supplier websites and catalogs
- **Contact information verification** for all operational suppliers

### 2. Hypergraph Data Enhancement
- Updated `RSNodes_updated.csv` with comprehensive supplier information
- Enhanced `supplier_capability_matrix.csv` with detailed research findings
- Added new data fields: website URLs, product counts, pricing availability, certifications
- Created timestamped backup files for data integrity

### 3. Database Schema Synchronization
- **Supabase integration**: Created normalized tables for suppliers, ingredients, edges, and capabilities
- **Neon database**: Established `skin_twin` schema with optimized table structures
- **JSON data exports**: Prepared structured data for easy database loading
- **Index optimization**: Added strategic indexes for query performance

### 4. Strategic Intelligence Gathering
- **High-priority suppliers identified**: Croda Chemicals, Carst & Walker, Botanichem, Meganede CC, Natchem CC
- **Supply chain risk assessment**: Categorized suppliers by risk level and strategic importance
- **New product identification**: Discovered innovative ingredients across multiple suppliers
- **Pricing transparency analysis**: Identified suppliers with public pricing vs. quote-based models
