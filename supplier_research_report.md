
# SKIN-TWIN Supplier Research Report

**Date:** 2025-09-23

**Author:** Manus AI

## 1. Executive Summary

This report provides a comprehensive analysis of 23 South African skincare ingredient suppliers within the SKIN-TWIN network. The research focused on assessing product availability, pricing, strategic importance, and supply chain risks. The findings from this research have been used to update the SKIN-TWIN hypergraph, providing a more accurate and dynamic view of the supply chain landscape. The key objective is to identify opportunities for cost optimization and mitigate supply chain risks.

Of the 23 suppliers researched, 20 are confirmed to be operational and available, while 3 have limited or unknown availability, requiring direct contact for verification. The research identified a total of over 2,400 skincare ingredients across the network, highlighting a rich and diverse local supply base. This report details the findings for each supplier and provides strategic recommendations for managing the supplier network effectively.
