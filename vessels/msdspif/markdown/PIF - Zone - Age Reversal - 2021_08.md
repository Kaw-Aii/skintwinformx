### A.5

Normal and reasonably foreseeable use

Zone Age Reversal Night Complex is an anti-aging product designed to visibly improve
the appearance of the hydration of the skin and make the signs of ageing less pronounced.
It is a leave-on product that in normal circumstances will be used once daily on the face
avoiding the eye area.
