### A.5

Normal and reasonably foreseeable use
Zone Daily Radiant Boost + βeta-Endorphin Stimulator is a product designed to
moisturize the skin, whilst providing protection from the drying, ageing effects of the
sun. It is a leave-on product that in normal circumstances will be used once daily on the
face and neck, avoiding the eye area.

• formulation
